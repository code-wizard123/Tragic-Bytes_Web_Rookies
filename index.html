About

Work-Connect is a Web App that helps daily-wage workers to connect with various clients in their locality. through this web app the client can post an issue/request.This will be a type of a platform for various clients who are in need of workers or vice versa.

Connecting Daily-wage Workers to Clients

In Work-Connect a Client can raise issues in many categories like Plumbing, Carpentry, Technical, Electrical and many more categories in order to find Workers to get theri job done.
